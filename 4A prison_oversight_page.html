The Critical Need for Oversight

Arizona's criminal legal system faces profound challenges that demand urgent attention and systematic reform. With zero independent oversight bodies monitoring Arizona's state prisons, transparency and accountability are critically lacking, allowing harmful conditions and ineffective rehabilitation efforts to persist unchecked.

This absence of oversight contributes to a corrections system that costs Arizona taxpayers over $1.5 billion annually while failing to meet basic standards of transparency and effectiveness.

Constitutional Violations

The ongoing Jensen v. Shinn lawsuit demonstrates the urgent need for independent oversight, with 34,500+ people in ADCRR experiencing constitutional violations related to healthcare, safety, and basic human dignity.

Our Mission

Praxis Initiative advocates for independent, external oversight of Arizona's prison system to ensure transparency, accountability, and the protection of human rights through a fully empowered correctional ombudsman.

Systemic Problems Without Oversight

The absence of independent monitoring creates conditions that harm incarcerated individuals, staff, and communities

⚕️

Healthcare Failures

Medical neglect and inadequate healthcare delivery leading to preventable deaths and constitutional violations across Arizona's prison system.

🚨

Staff Misconduct

Lack of independent investigation and accountability for correctional officer misconduct, contraband trafficking, and abuse of authority.

💰

Financial Waste

Costly litigation, settlements, and inefficient operations drain taxpayer resources without improving outcomes or safety.

📊

Data Transparency

Limited public access to performance data, incident reports, and outcome measurements prevents informed policy decisions.

🔄

Recidivism Rates

High reoffending rates indicate systemic failure to provide effective rehabilitation and reentry programming.

⚖️

Privatization Issues

Private prison operators operate with reduced oversight, creating perverse incentives and accountability gaps.

Independent Ombudsman Solution

Proven oversight model that creates accountability, improves conditions, and protects taxpayer investment

🔍

Investigation Authority

Independent power to investigate complaints, conduct facility inspections, and review policies without interference from corrections officials.

📋

Public Reporting

Regular public reports on conditions, incidents, and systemic issues with specific recommendations for improvement.

👥

Community Access

Direct communication channels for incarcerated individuals, families, and advocates to report concerns and seek assistance.

📈

Performance Monitoring

Continuous oversight of key performance indicators including safety, healthcare delivery, and rehabilitation outcomes.

🤝

Stakeholder Coordination

Bridge between corrections officials, legislative oversight, and community interests to ensure balanced accountability.

💡

Policy Recommendations

Evidence-based recommendations for systemic improvements, cost savings, and effective rehabilitation programming.
