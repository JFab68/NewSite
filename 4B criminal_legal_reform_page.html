Arizona's Sentencing Evolution

Understanding how we got here and where we need to go

Pre-1978

"Copper Time"

Indeterminate sentencing with early release based on rehabilitation progress. Lower incarceration rates and focus on treatment and reintegration.

1978-1993

Old Code Era

Transitional period with structured sentencing guidelines. Balanced approach between accountability and rehabilitation with moderate incarceration growth.

1994-Present

Truth-in-Sentencing

Mandatory minimum sentences and reduced discretion. Dramatic increase in prison population and costs, creating current mass incarceration crisis.
