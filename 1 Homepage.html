Why Praxis Matters

Our Mission

Transforming the Criminal Legal System

Praxis Initiative advocates for independent, external oversight of Arizona's prison system to ensure transparency, accountability, and the protection of human rights through a fully empowered correctional ombudsman.

Independent Prison Oversight

Advocating for a fully empowered correctional ombudsman to ensure transparency and accountability.

Drug Policy & Overdose Prevention

Advancing evidence‑based policies and providing community trainings to reduce overdose deaths.

Arts in Prison & Think Motion

Delivering music, visual arts, dance, and movement workshops inside correctional facilities.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered: who we are, what we do, and how you can help.

What is Praxis Initiative?

Praxis Initiative is Arizona's only directly impacted-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to transforming the criminal legal system by reducing mass incarceration, promoting transparency, and ensuring equal treatment under the law through independent prison oversight, harm reduction, and bipartisan policy solutions.

How can I get involved?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, volunteer with our advocacy campaigns, or support our work through donations. Every form of engagement helps strengthen our mission.

What programs does Praxis Initiative offer?

Our programs include independent prison oversight advocacy, drug policy & overdose prevention, arts in prison & think motion programming, criminal legal system reform, and civics & advocacy training for returning citizens.
